## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Omiage, a sole proprietorship registered in France and operating under the name Agent Studio (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”), collects, uses, and protects information when users access or use the website and interface (the “Service”).

The Service provides a web interface allowing users to create and manage on-chain profiles using the Intuition protocol deployed on the Intuition Mainnet blockchain network.

The Company operates the website interface but does not control the underlying blockchain protocol or network.

By accessing or using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

### Interface Operator

The Company:
- Develops the website interface
- Hosts the frontend
- Operates the web service
- Processes limited technical data required to run the website

### Blockchain Protocol

The Service interacts with the Intuition protocol, which is deployed on the Intuition Mainnet blockchain network.

The Company:
- Does not operate the blockchain network
- Does not control the Intuition protocol
- Cannot modify blockchain records.

All blockchain transactions are executed directly by users through their wallets.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:
- Email address (if you contact support)
- Messages or feedback you send
- Optional profile information submitted through the interface.

### 3.2 Wallet Information

When you use the Service, you may connect a cryptocurrency wallet.

We may process the public wallet address associated with your interaction with the Service.

Wallet addresses are public identifiers stored on blockchain networks.

### 3.3 Blockchain Data

The Service enables users to create or interact with on-chain profiles using the Intuition protocol.

Information recorded on the blockchain:
- Is public
- May be permanently stored
- Cannot be modified or deleted.

The Company does not control or alter blockchain data.

### 3.4 Authentication Providers

The Service may rely on third-party authentication providers such as Privy to enable secure login and wallet connection.

These providers may process certain information including:
- Wallet addresses
- Authentication tokens
- Device information.

Such processing is subject to the privacy policies of the respective providers.

### 3.5 Automatically Collected Information

When you access the Service, we may collect limited technical information such as:
- IP address
- Browser type
- Device information
- Operating system
- Pages visited
- Timestamps.

This information is used for:
- Security
- Service performance
- Analytics.
